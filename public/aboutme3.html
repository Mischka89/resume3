Remote Technical Support

In this role, I actively participated in remote technical support of IBM hardware and software products and/or systems. My responsibilities included

  • Providing remote troubleshooting and analysis assistance for installation (or re-installation), usage, and configuration questions.
  • Offering answers for general usage and operation questions.
  • Conducting problem determination and identifying the source of problems.
  • Reviewing diagnostic information to assist in isolating problem causes, which in-cluded interpreting traces and dumps.
  • Identifying known defects and recommending fixes to resolve problems.
  • Providing assistance on supported product known defects for which available corrective service information and program fixes were available.
  • Identifying suspected defects and engaging development teams to assist in resolution.
  • Assisting with questions regarding product documentation related to the sup-ported products.
  • Interpreting online manuals regarding IBM code and application interfaces.
  • Collaborating with other support centers and business units to provide seamless problem resolution.
  • Demonstrating proficiency in the hardware and software platform supported by maintaining applicable technical certifications.
  • Providing technical support service delivery within established guidelines; demonstrating soft skills and technical skills that contributed to client satisfac-tion.
  • Demonstrating excellent oral and written communication skills.
  • Fulfilling industry certification and skills training requirements, as needed for the position.
